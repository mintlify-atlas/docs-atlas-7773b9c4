## Overview

Postiz integrates with Instagram through the Facebook Graph API, allowing you to schedule posts, reels, stories, and carousel content to Instagram Business accounts.

<Warning>
  **Requirements:**
  - Instagram account must be a **Business Account**
  - Account must be connected to a **Facebook Page**
  - Not compatible with personal Instagram accounts
</Warning>

## Authentication

Instagram authentication is done through Facebook OAuth with specific Instagram permissions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Convert to Business Account">
    If not already done, convert your Instagram account to a Business Account in Instagram settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Link to Facebook Page">
    Connect your Instagram Business account to a Facebook Page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect via Postiz">
    Click "Connect Instagram" and authorize all requested permissions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Instagram Account">
    Choose which Instagram Business account to connect from your linked pages
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Required Permissions

- `instagram_basic` - Basic profile information
- `instagram_content_publish` - Post content
- `instagram_manage_comments` - Manage comments
- `instagram_manage_insights` - Access analytics
- `pages_show_list` - List connected pages
- `pages_read_engagement` - Read page engagement
- `business_management` - Business account management

## Supported Features

### Post Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Feed Posts">
    **Single Image/Video:**
    - Max caption: 2200 characters
    - Image: Up to 1080px, aspect ratio 4:5 to 1.91:1
    - Video: MP4 format, minimum 720p
    
    **Carousel:**
    - Multiple images (up to 10)
    - All images must have compatible aspect ratios
    - Cannot mix images and videos in carousel
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Reels">
    - Video only (MP4 format)
    - Vertical format recommended (9:16)
    - Minimum 720p resolution
    - Optional trial reels for testing
    - Thumbnail offset selection supported
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Stories">
    - Single image or video
    - 9:16 aspect ratio recommended
    - Auto-disappears after 24 hours
    - Cannot schedule carousels as stories
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Media Requirements

<Warning>
  **Instagram requires at least one attachment.** Text-only posts are not supported. Stories can have only one picture or video.
</Warning>

#### Image Specifications
- **Formats**: JPG, PNG (automatically converted)
- **Resolution**: Max 1920x1080px, minimum 320px
- **Aspect Ratio**: Between 4:5 and 1.91:1
- **Size**: Under 8MB recommended

#### Video Specifications
- **Format**: MP4
- **Resolution**: Minimum 720p
- **Aspect Ratio**: Flexible, but 9:16 for Reels and Stories
- **Duration**: Up to 60 seconds for feed, 90 seconds for Reels

## Post Processing

Instagram posts go through several processing stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Media Upload">
    Media files are uploaded to Instagram servers
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Processing">
    Instagram processes the media (status: IN_PROGRESS)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publication">
    Once processing completes, the post is published
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Processing can take 30+ seconds per media item. Postiz automatically waits for processing to complete.
</Info>

- Reach
- Engagement

- Likes
- Comments
- Shares

## Error Handling

Common Instagram errors and solutions:

| Error Code | Error | Solution |
|------------|-------|----------|
| 2207001 | Spam detected | Change content to avoid spam filters |
| 2207009 | Invalid aspect ratio | Resize to 4:5 - 1.91:1 range |
| 2207042 | Daily limit reached | Max 25 posts/day - wait until tomorrow |
| 36001 | Resolution too high | Max 1920x1080px |
| 190 | Missing permissions | Reconnect account with all permissions |
| 2207081 | Trial reels not supported | Account doesn't support trial reels |

<Warning>
  **Permission Errors**: If you see error 190, you need to reconnect your account and ensure all permissions are granted during the OAuth flow.
</Warning>

## Rate Limits

<Info>
  **Instagram Limits:**
  - Maximum **25 posts per day** per account
  - Maximum 400 concurrent jobs (generous limit)
  - Page rate limits may apply for high-volume posting
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account Not Found">
    If your Instagram account isn't showing:
    - Verify it's a Business account
    - Ensure it's connected to a Facebook Page
    - Check that the connected Page is accessible
    - Try reconnecting both Facebook and Instagram
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Media Upload Failed">
    Common media upload issues:
    - Check aspect ratio is within 4:5 to 1.91:1
    - Verify resolution doesn't exceed 1920x1080
    - Ensure file format is supported (JPG/PNG/MP4)
    - Try re-uploading or converting the file
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Collaborator Tags Not Working">
    Collaborator limitations:
    - Not supported for carousel posts
    - Not supported for story posts
    - Other account must approve the collaboration
    - Only works with Instagram Business accounts
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Token Expired">
    Instagram tokens expire after 60 days:
    - Postiz automatically refreshes tokens
    - If refresh fails, manually reconnect
    - Ensure Facebook permissions are still granted
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
