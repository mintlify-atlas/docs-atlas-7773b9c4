## Authentication Methods

Postiz uses secure OAuth 2.0 authentication for most platforms. Some platforms require additional setup:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="OAuth 2.0 Platforms">
    Most platforms (X, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest, Reddit, Threads) use standard OAuth 2.0 flows. Simply click "Connect" and authorize the application.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="App Password Platforms">
    Bluesky requires an app password instead of OAuth. Generate an app password from your Bluesky settings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Bot Token Platforms">
    Discord and Slack use bot tokens configured at the application level.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Custom Instance Platforms">
    Mastodon supports custom instance URLs for self-hosted or alternative servers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Rate Limits & Restrictions

Each platform has different rate limits and posting restrictions:

<Warning>
  **Important Rate Limits:**
  - **X**: 300 posts per 3 hours (very strict)
  - **Instagram**: 25 posts per day maximum
  - **Reddit**: 1 post per second recommended
  - **LinkedIn**: Professional posting limits apply
  - **TikTok**: 5 pending posts maximum in inbox
</Warning>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to Settings → Integrations in your Postiz dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Platform">
    Choose the social platform you want to connect
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Follow the OAuth flow or enter credentials as required
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Posting">
    Create your first scheduled post from the calendar view
  </Step>
</Steps>
