## What is Postiz?

Postiz is your all-in-one AI social media scheduling tool that helps you manage posts, build an audience, capture leads, and grow your business across 30+ platforms. Whether you're a solopreneur, marketing team, or enterprise, Postiz provides everything you need to streamline your social media presence.

**Open-source alternative to:** Buffer.com, Hypefury, Twitter Hunter, and more.

## Supported Platforms

Postiz connects to all major social networks and communication platforms:

**Social Media:** X (Twitter), LinkedIn (Personal & Pages), Facebook, Instagram, Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon, Farcaster, Nostr, VK

**Video Platforms:** YouTube, TikTok, Kick, Twitch

**Design & Creative:** Dribbble, Pinterest

**Blogging:** Medium, Dev.to, Hashnode, WordPress

**Communication:** Discord, Slack, Telegram

**Community:** Reddit, Lemmy, Skool, Whop, Moltbook

**Business:** Google My Business, Listmonk (Newsletter)

<Note>
  Each platform requires its own API credentials for OAuth authentication. See our [installation guide](/installation) for setup details.
</Note>

## Technology Stack

Postiz is built with modern, scalable technologies:

- **Frontend:** Next.js 14 with React 18, TailwindCSS 3
- **Backend:** NestJS with TypeScript
- **Orchestration:** Temporal for reliable workflow execution
- **Database:** PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
- **Cache:** Redis for session management
- **Monorepo:** PNPM workspaces
